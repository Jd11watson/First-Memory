About — Molly Watson

A small studio with
a careful hand.

MW Designs is a one-woman studio led by Molly Watson, based in Bryan / College Station, Texas. The work spans residential interiors, full-home remodels, and a handful of commercial projects each year — always small, always considered.

Every project gets Molly's attention from first walk-through to final styling. No account managers, no junior designers. Just a careful eye and a long, useful conversation about how you want to live.

Process

How a project unfolds.

01

Conversation

We meet at your place — coffee, walk-through, and a long conversation about how the space should feel and function.

02

Concept

I return with a focused direction: palette, materials, and a few pieces of furniture pinned down. No mood-board overwhelm.

03

Plans & Sourcing

Specifications, drawings, vendor coordination. I handle the procurement, contractors, and the small daily decisions.

04

Install & Style

Delivery week. Furniture in, art hung, last details. I leave the house ready to live in — not staged.
